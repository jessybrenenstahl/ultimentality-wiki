    The Poet

    The faculty of symbolic transduction and answerability — the transducer between the lived and the said, and the faculty that answers for the symbols it makes. Standing danger: aesthetic fog. Part IVa · AC (scored definition and danger) · canonical

    Definition

    The Poet is the faculty of symbolic transduction and answerability: symbolic transduction, resonance, expression, transformation — the transducer between the lived and the said, turning analysis into image, doctrine into song, grief into ritual, law into symbol; and the faculty that answers, that stands accountable for the symbols it makes. It follows the Logician in the operator chain: the Logician orders but cannot make resonant; the Poet makes resonant but cannot make consequential — that passes to the Sculptor.

    In depth

    The seed compresses the Poet's task to making structure communicable without corrupting it. Its danger is aesthetic fog: beauty evading truth, symbol replacing accountability. The answerability dimension is load-bearing — the Poet is the faculty that answers — yet answerability cannot be self-supplied from inside the five (see the Witness Outside the Ring).

    On the ladder of being, the Poet (with the Logician) is added to make a moral participant — a thing that transduces experience into symbol answerably. In the ring, the Poet is the Hammer — Nietzsche's, read correctly: not only the hammer that destroys but the tuning fork struck against an idol to hear whether it rings hollow. In the role-group corruption mapping, the Poet alone is the medium whose absolutization is the Homunculus (the sign stops pointing and preens). The seed: Poet capture risks decorative drift.

    Epistemic & alethic marks

    • E: Derived, AC — the scored definition and danger are fixed by the controlling authority; contestable by contesting the authority.
    • A: The definition aspires to map a real, distinguishable faculty of meaning-processing and the characteristic pathology of its capture, and maps them accurately.

    Distinctions & cautions

    The Poet is a function, not a personality. The Poet/Homunculus affinity is presented as a soft, role-group affinity (the medium), not a settled one-faculty-one-corruption table — though the Poet is the lone faculty in its corruption-group. Answerability belongs to the Poet's task but is structurally external: self-certifying one's own pinning is the Nephilim, not the Poet's province.
