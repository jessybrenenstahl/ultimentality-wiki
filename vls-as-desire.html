    VLS as Desire

    VLS made active: truth and freedom are held as corrigible semblance, and the mark of holding them rightly is the active desire to be proven wrong. Part I · treatise-side (derived through the Telos)

    Definition

    A modern development makes VLS active and operable. It is no longer only a stance but a desire: truth and freedom are held as corrigible semblance, and the mark of holding them rightly is the active desire to be proven wrong. This development is treatise-side, derived through the Telos.

    In depth

    The argument is end-directed. Being refuted where one is wrong is the only way the carried structure improves, and improving the structure is continuation — which is the end (Part VI). To cling to a claim against the evidence is to choose the local comfort of being-right over the propagation of better structure: i.e., to choose against one's own end.

    VLS is thereby the corpus's antibody against its own systematicity. It is the reason this treatise carries a contestability discipline, a falsification program, and a record of its own errors. The apparatus is the desire made operable.

    This active form is also what gives the integrity rule its teeth: silently promoting a claim from a more-contestable tier to a less-contestable one — and above all, promoting any Derived claim into "forced/foundational" — is itself a breaking of the framework. That is the textual Nephilim, self-certification committed on the page.

    Epistemic & alethic marks

    • E: Derived, treatise-side.
    • A: Maps the operable form of the no-possession condition; the desire-to-be-refuted is what accuracy-aspiration feels like from inside a system that cannot possess its object.

    Distinctions & cautions

    • This is treatise-side, not the canonical core of VLS — it is the active development of VLS derived through the Telos, not the seed/benchmark common claim.
    • The desire to be proven wrong is not skepticism for its own sake: it is instrumental to continuation, the end. Refutation improves the carried structure; improvement is continuation.
    • The "apparatus" (contestability discipline, falsification program, error record) is the desire made operable — it is not decoration but the working form of the desire.
