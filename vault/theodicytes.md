# The Theodicytes

> **The corpus's accounting of how a good architecture generates its own evils from within rather than importing them — exactly three named corruptions, each a term of the act absolutized.**
> *Part V · AC (named three, genus, term-mappings) / CV (the count) · canonical*

## Definition
The Theodicytes are the framework's three named corruptions: the **Spectre**, the **Nephilim**, and the **Homunculus**. They are not evils imported from outside the architecture but pathologies the architecture produces *from within* — each one a single term of the act inflated past its station. The corpus names and scores exactly three, with their role coalitions; the *closure* claim that three is complete is held separately, lower down.

## In depth
The seed states the corruptions in a **primitive-domination** form: each corruption named by which directional primitive over-dominates while the others are suppressed. This edition re-derives the **genus** ([[absolutization|absolutization]]) and holds the corruption mechanism at the authority-canonical tier, while holding the *count* at the carving tier.

Each named corruption is **one of the three terms of symbolic participation absolutized**, where symbolic participation carves into *a self engaging a world through a medium (the symbol): who participates, what it engages, by what means.*

- **[[spectre|Spectre]]** — the world-relation absolutized (under Fear). Coalition: Warden + Sculptor.
- **[[nephilim|Nephilim]]** — the self absolutized. Coalition: Captive + Logician.
- **[[homunculus|Homunculus]]** — the medium absolutized. Coalition: Poet.

Most prior version-churn came from one over-reach: trying to **force-fuse the five SPLCW role-captures onto the three Theodicytes as a clean partition.** That partition is not in the source, and this edition does not make it. The role → Theodicyte mapping is a **coalition relation, not an identity** ([[role-corruption-affinities|role–corruption affinities]]), kept as soft, contestable, non-exhaustive affinity.

## Epistemic & alethic marks
- **E:** Derived, AC for the three named corruptions, each one's mapping to a term, and the genus; CV for the count.
- **A:** Each definition aspires to map a real, recurrent, self-maintaining pathology — world-seizure/sealing, self-inflation, medium-preening — the framework's central and best-defended pathologies, mapped accurately. The closure-at-three does not map exhaustiveness.

## Distinctions & cautions
The three are **not absences but presences in the wrong place** — never privations. The *count* of three is a contestable carving, symmetric with the four-primitive and two-Force sets; it is exposed by the [[the-2026-adversarial-run|2026 run]]'s counter-instances ([[installed-compulsion|installed-compulsion]], [[answerable-optimizer|answerable optimizer]]). There is no fourth *captured-propagation* corruption ([[no-fourth-argument|the no-fourth argument]]), but that sub-result does not secure the count. Do not partition the five role-captures into the three: **five does not factor into three** ([[two-layer-separation|two-layer separation]]).
