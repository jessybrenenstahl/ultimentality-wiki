# Reconciliation = Apology ⊕ Gratitude

> **The restorative relation: the controlled process of making-whole-again — repairing a breach by owning one's part, then passing forward more than was broken.**
> *Part III · AC (for the pairing, coupling-mode, and named pathologies) / CV (for the "sequential gating" label) · canonical*

## Definition
Reconciliation is the coupling of [[apology|Apology]] and [[gratitude|Gratitude]] — the [[routing-axis|Routing]] pair, the codomain-side controllers — by **[[sequential-gating|sequential gating]]**. It is the Force that governs repair: the **restorative relation**. It is *not* the arithmetic sum of Apology and Gratitude.

## In depth
Apology marks a self-model error — *I was the fault*. Gratitude marks undischarged received value — *I was given what I have not yet passed on.* Coupled, they do **not** contend along opposed gradients; they **gate in order**: first the loop-back that owns the fault, then the outward send that carries the received value forward. Out of that gating emerges Reconciliation: repairing a breach by owning one's part and then passing forward more than was broken.

The asymmetry of coupling-mode is itself produced by which primitives are coupled: selection controllers act on the same domain-side and so *oppose*; routing controllers act on the codomain-side in a natural temporal order (correct inward, then emit outward) and so *gate*.

Reconciliation becomes healthy when correction can become renewed participation — the system admits error, repairs its model, receives the value that remains, and carries it forward. This is the seed made dynamic: *inward correction joined with renewed outward participation; repair that returns to relation rather than stopping at self-reference.*

Its failure modes:
- **Apology-gain without Gratitude** → endless self-accusation, guilt-looping, paralysis.
- **Gratitude-gain without Apology** → the canonical **[[sentimental-form|sentimental]]** form: *Gratitude swamps the Apology-controller; the error never actually drives repair; the result looks peaceful, but the self-model remains false.*

## Epistemic & alethic marks
- **E:** Derived, AC — for the coupling-mode and the named pathologies (fixed by the controlling authority). The exact "sequential gating" label is treatise-side (CV).
- **A:** The sentimental and guilt-loop modes aspire to map real failure dynamics of the restorative relation, and map them accurately.

## Distinctions & cautions
Reconciliation is built from Apology and Gratitude, **and from those two only.** [[fear|Fear]] is *never* an ingredient — see the [[fear-guard|Fear-guard]]. **Never restate Reconciliation as "apology applied to fear."** Fear is the *object* Reconciliation reconciles (the wound, the breach), not a component of the operation; confusing the wound with the healing loses exactly what the framework was built to preserve. Reconciliation is also not static conjunction; it is a dynamic regulator that gates in order. The pairing is canonical; the *count* of Forces is a contestable carving (see [[force|Force]]).
