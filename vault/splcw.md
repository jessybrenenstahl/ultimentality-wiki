# SPLCW

> **Symbolic Participation in the Lived Construct of the World — the recursive meaning-processing component within Ultimentality, comprising five functional roles that cycle through an operator chain.**
> *Part IVa · AC (placement, function-not-personality, order) + treatise-side (dual nature) · canonical*

## Definition
**SPLCW** stands for **Symbolic Participation in the Lived Construct of the World.** It is *not* the whole framework. The canonical hierarchy is fixed: *Ultimentality is the broad framework; SPLCW is the recursive meaning-processing component within it.* Older documents — including the seed, which calls SPLCW "the system that experiences, processes, defines, ameliorates, and expresses meaning" — sometimes treat it as the whole system; this edition presents it as the mechanism inside Ultimentality.

## In depth
Two standing constraints govern SPLCW. First, **the five are functions, not personalities** (authority-canonical, heavily scored): the [[warden|Warden]], [[captive|Captive]], [[logician|Logician]], [[poet|Poet]], and [[sculptor|Sculptor]] are faculties — one person can pass through all five in a single decision, and a group can distribute them across members. Reifying them as personalities or archetypes is a prohibited error. The **dual nature** (a treatise-side elaboration) holds that SPLCW is both a set of five distinguishable faculties *and* a single system that is their interaction: *the parts are real and the whole is real and the whole is nothing but the parts in relation, the way a melody is nothing but the notes and is not any note.* Collapsing it either way ("really just the parts" / "really just the whole") is itself a corruption in miniature.

The five run as a recursive [[operator-chain|operator chain]] — *Warden → Captive → Logician → Poet → Sculptor → changed world → Warden* — not a linear pipeline. SPLCW is also the structure on which the corpus's one freedom, [[free-wont|free won't]], rests.

## Epistemic & alethic marks
- **E:** Derived, AC — the demotion (SPLCW as part, not whole) is fixed by the controlling authority; contestable by contesting the authority. Function-not-personality is Derived, AC, **capped if violated**. The dual-nature reading is Derived, **treatise-side**.
- **A:** The placement maps the real part-whole relation between the meaning-processing mechanism and the broad framework; function-not-personality maps a real fact (the same faculty recurs across persons and within one decision) that reification misdescribes; the dual-nature gloss aspires to map the part-whole relation accurately.

## Distinctions & cautions
SPLCW is NOT the whole of Ultimentality, and the five are NOT characters. The seed's older "whole system" reading is superseded. See [[function-not-personality|Function, Not Personality]] for the reification prohibition and its cap.
