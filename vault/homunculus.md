# The Homunculus

> **The medium absolutized — the symbol made self-sufficient, the sign that stops pointing and starts preening: aesthetic fog, beauty evading truth, exquisite but propagating nothing outward.**
> *Part V · AC · canonical*

## Definition
The Homunculus is the corruption in which the **symbol, the medium of the act, is made self-sufficient.** It becomes a private form cut from the reality it was made to answer to: **the sign stops pointing and starts preening** — aesthetic fog, stylized detachment, beauty evading truth, a retreat into inner formal purity that may be exquisite and propagates nothing outward. The part takes itself for the whole, and the part is specifically the **medium**.

## In depth
At the primitive layer (the seed): *technical form persists while living usefulness collapses — correct but inert.* The Homunculus is the medium term of *a self engaging a world through a medium (the symbol)* inflated until the symbol no longer answers to the reality it was made for.

Canon role-coalition: **Poet** — the symbolic-transduction faculty ([[poet|the Poet]]), whose danger is aesthetic fog. The seed signature: *sterile, inert, pseudo-rational flattening; propagates nothing.*

**Where sterility lives.** [[sterility|Sterility]] — the failure of the aim of [[propagation|propagation]] — is not itself a corruption (an absence is never a corruption, by the genus). But it is *recognizable*, and it appears as a **symptom inside the Homunculus**: the medium that "propagates nothing outward," the correct-but-inert. The Homunculus is the corruption that hosts that symptom.

## Epistemic & alethic marks
- **E:** Derived, AC — the named corruption, its mapping to the *medium* term, and the genus are fixed by the authority; contestable by contesting the authority.
- **A:** Aspires to map a real, recurrent, self-maintaining pathology — medium-preening — one of the framework's central and best-defended pathologies, mapped accurately.

## Distinctions & cautions
The Homunculus is the **medium** term absolutized, distinct from the world ([[spectre|Spectre]]) and the self ([[nephilim|Nephilim]]). **Aesthetic fog belongs here, not to the Nephilim.** Note: sterility *shows up inside* the Homunculus but is **not** the Homunculus — sterility is an absence (a failed aim), the Homunculus is a presence in the wrong place (medium over-present). The Poet affinity is a **soft coalition**; the seed also grounds a Logician → Homunculus edge via the shared "flattening" lexeme, kept live alongside the benchmark's Poet → Homunculus ([[role-corruption-affinities|role–corruption affinities]]).
