# Gratitude = (propagation, undischarged received value)

> **Propagation that discharges undischarged received value — the discrepancy between value received and value carried forward.**
> *Part II · AC · canonical*

## Definition
**Gratitude = (propagation, undischarged received value).** It is a [[predicate-binding|predicate binding]]: the primitive [[propagation|propagation]] bound under the regulated error signal **undischarged received value**. Gratitude is propagation that *discharges undischarged received value* — the discrepancy between value received and value carried forward. The controller drives that discrepancy toward zero.

## In depth
Gratitude is the binding **B(propagation) = (propagation, undischarged received value)**. The actuator is the directional operator [[propagation|propagation]] (emits transformed structure into the field, another system, a medium, a trace, or a future state); the controller is named "Gratitude"; the error signal is undischarged received value.

The framework names the contrast directly: **Ingratitude hoards it; Gratitude discharges it into continuation.** The accuracy discipline holds — extract the regulated error (undischarged received value), not the surface delivery channel.

Gratitude is a cornerstone binding and an ingredient of [[reconciliation|Reconciliation = Apology ⊕ Gratitude]], coupled with [[apology|Apology]] by [[sequential-gating|sequential gating]]. Its canonical failure is the [[sentimental-form|sentimental form]], in which Gratitude swamps Apology and the self-model stays false.

## Epistemic & alethic marks
- **E:** Derived, AC — fixed verbatim by the controlling authority; contestable by contesting the authority.
- **A:** The binding aspires to map the regulated error actually driving the named emotion; extract the regulated error, not the surface delivery channel.

## Distinctions & cautions
Gratitude is NOT a primitive (treating it as one is [[cardinal-error|the cardinal error]]). Ingratitude is the hoarding of received value; Gratitude is its discharge into continuation. The binding is regulation toward a setpoint, not preservation of an invariant.
