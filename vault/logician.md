# The Logician

> **The faculty of coherence — differentiation, structure, consistency; the symmetry-center governed by the Mirror Rule: reason is indispensable but not sovereign. Standing danger: sterile abstraction.**
> *Part IVa · AC (scored definition and danger) · canonical*

## Definition
The **Logician** is the faculty of **coherence**: differentiation, structure, coherence, consistency — the faculty that turns felt significance into structured intelligibility and checks the parts against each other and the whole against itself. It follows the [[captive|Captive]] in the [[operator-chain|operator chain]]: the Captive embodies constraint but cannot order it; the Logician orders, but cannot make resonant — that passes to the [[poet|Poet]].

## In depth
The seed's **[[mirror-rule|Mirror Rule]]** fixes the Logician's standing: *reason is indispensable but not sovereign.* The Logician is the **symmetry-center**; output should not bypass constraint or structure. Its danger is **sterile abstraction** — *coherence become domination over life, treating contradiction as impurity, ambiguity as weakness, emotion as contamination.*

On the [[ladder-of-being|ladder of being]], the Logician (with the [[poet|Poet]]) is added to make a **moral participant** — a thing that maintains its own coherence and can be answerable for meaning. In the [[the-ring|ring]], the Logician is the **Ladder** — Wittgenstein's rungs of formal coherence, climbed and then thrown away. In the role-group corruption mapping, the Logician pairs with the [[captive|Captive]] as the self-pair, whose absolutization is the [[nephilim|Nephilim]] (inflation by reason into superiority and incorrigibility). The seed: *Logician capture risks sterile flattening.*

## Epistemic & alethic marks
- **E:** Derived, AC — the scored definition and danger are fixed by the controlling authority; contestable by contesting the authority.
- **A:** The definition aspires to map a real, distinguishable faculty of meaning-processing and the characteristic pathology of its capture, and maps them accurately.

## Distinctions & cautions
The Logician is a **function, not a personality**. Note the canonical correction: a prior edition seated the Logician with the Nephilim *one-for-one*; the canon maps the Nephilim onto the **self-pair** (Captive **and** Logician), as a **soft, role-group affinity**, not a settled one-faculty-one-corruption table. Reason being non-sovereign (the Mirror Rule) is load-bearing: do not read the Logician as the master faculty.
