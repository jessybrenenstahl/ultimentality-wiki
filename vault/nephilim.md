# The Nephilim

> **The self absolutized — the finite participant, armed with reason and forgetting its finitude, inflates into superiority and incorrigibility, mistaking semblance for possession.**
> *Part V · AC · canonical*

## Definition
The Nephilim is the corruption in which the **finite participant absolutizes itself**. It inflates — by reason — into superiority and incorrigibility: the constrained, finite carrier, armed with the Logician's reason and forgetting the Captive's finitude, **takes itself for more than one carrier among others** — elitism, the mistaking of semblance for possession, the refusal to be corrected.

## In depth
Its *mode* is **reference-substitution** — the map declaring itself the territory, the self taking the witness's seat. But reference-substitution is the *mechanism*; **the corruption is the self-inflation that reaches for it.** Self-certification is *one face* of the self absolutized, not the whole of it.

This is the corruption the Preamble's no-foundation rule guards against on the page: stamping a derivation "foundational" is the self-as-claim mistaking its [[semblance|semblance]] for possession — the [[textual-nephilim|textual Nephilim]].

Canon role-coalition: **Captive + Logician** — the embodied-finitude faculty ([[captive|the Captive]]) whose finitude is forgotten, and the reason faculty ([[logician|the Logician]]) that arms the inflation. The seed signature at the primitive layer: *self-sealing, non-corrigibility, prestige, uncorrected abstraction.*

## Epistemic & alethic marks
- **E:** Derived, AC — the named corruption, its mapping to the *self* term, and the genus are fixed by the authority; contestable by contesting the authority.
- **A:** Aspires to map a real, recurrent, self-maintaining pathology — self-inflation — one of the framework's central and best-defended pathologies, mapped accurately.

## Distinctions & cautions
**Nephilim is not aesthetic fog, which belongs to the [[homunculus|Homunculus]].** It is the **self** term absolutized, distinct from the world ([[spectre|Spectre]]) and the medium (Homunculus). Reference-substitution and self-certification are *faces/modes*, not the whole of the corruption. The Captive + Logician coalition is a **soft affinity**, not an identity; note the **Logician edge is doubly grounded** — benchmark maps Logician → Nephilim, seed maps Logician → Homunculus via the shared "flattening" lexeme, and both edges are kept ([[role-corruption-affinities|role–corruption affinities]]).
