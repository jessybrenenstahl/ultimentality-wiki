# Coalescence-by-Non-Contradictory-Fit

> **The sturdier evidential pillar: Ultimentality was not architected top-down but coalesced from independently-built systems that cohered by fitting together — held as strong corroboration, not proof.**
> *Part VII · CV · treatise-side*

## Definition
Coalescence-by-non-contradictory-fit is the framework's main evidential argument. Ultimentality was **not architected top-down.** It is the coalescence of many independently-built systems that cohered purely by fitting together — non-contradictory, mutually reinforcing, sense-making. The claim is that cross-domain, load-bearing, non-contradictory fit is much harder to manufacture than fit within a single domain.

## In depth
The four disparate domains are: a theology of roles ([[splcw|SPLCW]]), a control-theory of emotion (the primitives and the Forces), an epistemics of semblance ([[vls|VLS]]), and a memory architecture ([[transparentocracy|Transparentocracy]] / the externalized-memory OS). A single mind can fabricate *some* internal consistency; it is far harder to fabricate non-contradictory, mutually load-bearing fit *across* four disparate domains. This is offered as the internal analogue of the external convergence of the 2026 local-first memory-system genre onto the same structures.

The argument is held, explicitly and without inflation, as **strong corroboration, not proof.** It is sturdier than precedence or date claims (which cannot be independently verified), but it is corroboration, not demonstration. It is consistent with — and stronger than — the treatise's own bound on itself: this is *not a discovered structure of the world but the hardened articulation of one way of construing it.* By its own lights the framework lands in the same boat as physics: an accurate-aspiring construal that does not own its object.

## Epistemic & alethic marks
- **E:** Derived, treatise-side CV — corroboration, contestable by counter-instance or by declining the inference from fit to evidential weight.
- **A:** the argument aspires to map a real evidential asymmetry — cross-domain fit is harder to fabricate than single-domain fit — and holds itself to corroboration, not possession of proof.

## Distinctions & cautions
It is NOT proof and NOT demonstration — the word is *corroboration*. It does not claim a discovered structure of the world, only a hardened articulation of one construal. The whole point of shifting weight here (and away from dated precedence) is to avoid the [[textual-nephilim|textual Nephilim]]: a self-certified "strongest evidence" superlative is forbidden, so the weight rests on fit, held at CV.
