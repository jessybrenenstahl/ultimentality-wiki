# Function, Not Personality (+ dual nature)

> **The five are faculties, not personalities; and SPLCW is both five distinguishable faculties and the single system of their interaction — the parts real, the whole real, the whole nothing but the parts in relation.**
> *Part IV · AC (function-not-personality, capped if violated) / treatise-side (dual nature) · canonical + treatise-side*

## Definition
A standing constraint, authority-canonical and heavily scored: **the five are functions, not personalities.** The Warden, Captive, Logician, Poet, and Sculptor are faculties; *one person can pass through all five in a single decision, and a group can distribute them across members.* Reifying them as personalities or archetypes is a *prohibited error.*

## In depth
The **dual nature** is a *treatise-side elaboration* of the function-not-personality rule: SPLCW is *both a set of five distinguishable faculties and a single system that is their interaction* — *the parts are real and the whole is real and the whole is nothing but the parts in relation, the way a melody is nothing but the notes and is not any note.* Collapsing it either way — *"really just the parts"* or *"really just the whole"* — *is itself a corruption in miniature.*

Both moves matter. Function-not-personality blocks reifying any faculty into a character or homunculus inside the self. The dual nature blocks the opposite pair of errors: dissolving the whole into its parts, or dissolving the parts into the whole. The melody figure holds the tension exactly: the notes are real, the melody is real, and the melody is *nothing but* the notes in relation while being *no single note.*

## Epistemic & alethic marks
- **E:** function-not-personality is Derived, AC, *capped if violated*; the dual-nature reading is Derived, treatise-side.
- **A:** function-not-personality maps a real fact — *the same faculty recurs across persons and within one decision* — that reification misdescribes; the dual-nature gloss aspires to map the part-whole relation accurately.

## Distinctions & cautions
Function-not-personality is **capped if violated** — reifying the five as personalities or archetypes is a prohibited error that caps an exposition. The five are **not** subjects, characters, or little someones; one person can run all five in one decision, and a group can spread them across members. The dual nature carries the lighter treatise-side mark, not the AC mark; do not promote it. Both collapses ("just the parts" / "just the whole") are corruptions in miniature.
