    Sequential Gating

    Reconciliation's coupling-mode: Apology and Gratitude do not contend but gate in order — first own the fault, then carry the value outward. Part III · AC (for the coupling-mode itself) / CV (for the exact label) · canonical coupling-mode, treatise-side label

    Definition

    Sequential gating is the specific regulatory relation by which Apology and Gratitude couple into Reconciliation. Unlike Submission's contention, the two controllers do not contend along opposed gradients; they gate in order: first the loop-back that owns the fault, then the outward send that carries the received value forward.

    In depth

    Apology marks a self-model error (I was the fault); Gratitude marks undischarged received value (I was given what I have not yet passed on). Coupled, they fire in a natural temporal order — correct inward first, then emit outward. Out of that gating emerges the controlled process of making-whole-again: repairing a breach by owning one's part and then passing forward more than was broken.

    The asymmetry with opposed-gradient contention is itself produced by which primitives are coupled: Routing controllers act on the codomain-side in a natural temporal order (correct inward, then emit outward) and so gate, where Selection controllers act on the same domain-side and so oppose.

    Because the coupling is ordered rather than opposed, its pathologies are gating failures: Apology-gain without Gratitude (the gate never opens — endless self-accusation, guilt-looping, paralysis) and Gratitude-gain without Apology (the first gate is skipped — the sentimental form, where the error never drives repair).

    Epistemic & alethic marks

    • E: Derived, AC for the coupling-mode (that Reconciliation couples by sequential gating is canonical); the exact phrase "sequential gating" is a treatise-side carving (CV), contestable by counter-instance.
    • A: The gating reading aspires to map how the restorative relation actually behaves — ordered inward-then-outward repair and its failure modes — and maps it accurately where static conjunction does not.

    Distinctions & cautions

    Gating is not contention: the controllers do not push against each other simultaneously; they fire in sequence. The order is load-bearing — own the fault before carrying value outward; skipping the first gate yields the sentimental form. Gating is also not addition. The label is the writer's scaffolding; the coupling-mode it names is fixed by the authority.
