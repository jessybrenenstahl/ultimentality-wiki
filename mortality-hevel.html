    Mortality / Hevel

    Qoheleth's hevel — the carrier's ending, the pressure that makes carrying urgent: the Telos's activating condition, kept strictly distinct from its reflection-surface, the nihil. Part VI · AC · canonical

    Definition

    Qoheleth's hevel is mortality — the carrier's ending, the pressure that makes carrying urgent. It is the activating condition of the Telos, not its reflection-surface. Mortality remains the activating pressure it always was; the framework's correction is simply to stop conflating it with The Nihil.

    In depth

    Through earlier editions, the reflection-surface of the Telos was taken to be the void / oblivion / death — Qoheleth's hevel. The canon corrects this by splitting two outsides that the prior edition ran together. Mortality (hevel) is a pressure that intensifies meaning — the activating condition that makes the propagation of meaning-bearing structure urgent. The nihil is the unexperienced structured signification against which carried meaning is measured — the reflection-surface. These are two genuinely different roles: one activates, the other completes-by-reflection.

    This split conforms to the canon's own care, which distinguishes mortality (a pressure that intensifies meaning) from the structured-signification ontology. The mortality/nihil distinction maps two genuinely different roles accurately; mortality is what makes carrying matter, the nihil is what the carrying is held against.

    Epistemic & alethic marks

    • E: The mortality/nihil split is Derived, AC — contestable by contesting the authority.
    • A: The distinction maps two genuinely different roles accurately: mortality as the pressure that intensifies meaning, the nihil as the unexperienced structure the carried meaning is measured against.

    Distinctions & cautions

    • Not the reflection-surface — mortality is the activating condition. The reflection-surface is the nihil. Conflating the two is the prior edition's error the canon corrects.
    • Not death-as-completion — mortality is the urgency that drives carrying, not what carried meaning is measured against.
    • Mortality "remains the activating pressure it always was" — the correction reclassifies it, it does not demote it.
