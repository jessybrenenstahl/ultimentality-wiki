    The Trapped Form

    Submission's canonical failure: a participant who submits without Reconciliation's corrective work becomes unable to exit the regulator — the actuator locks. Part III · AC · canonical (named pathology)

    Definition

    The trapped form is the canonical failure mode of Submission. A participant who submits without the corrective and transmissive work of Reconciliation may become unable to exit the regulator: Fear says leaving destroys safety; Love says leaving destroys relation; the actuator locks. This is a control failure, not captured by static conjunction.

    In depth

    Because Submission couples Love and Fear by opposed-gradient contention, its pathologies are imbalances or lock-ups of that contention rather than additive sums. The trapped form is the lock-up case: both opposed gradients simultaneously hold the actuator shut. Fear's away-gradient says exit destroys safety; Love's toward-gradient says exit destroys relation. Neither releases, so the participant cannot leave — the regulator becomes a trap.

    The diagnosis is precise about the cure: the trap arises specifically when Submission operates without Reconciliation's corrective and transmissive work. The vertical relation, uncoupled from the restorative relation, has no path out of its own lock-up. This places the trapped form alongside Submission's gain-dominance failures — Fear-gain dominant (servility, panic, coerced obedience, domination internalized) and Love-gain dominant without boundary (self-erasure, dependency, boundary collapse) — as one of three named pathologies of the Force.

    Epistemic & alethic marks

    • E: Derived, AC — a named pathology fixed by the controlling authority (the same authority that fixes Submission's coupling-mode and pathologies). The "opposed-gradient contention" label that frames it is treatise-side (CV).
    • A: The trapped form aspires to map a real failure dynamic of the vertical relation, and maps it accurately.

    Distinctions & cautions

    The trapped form is not captured by static conjunction — it is a control failure, a lock-up of opposed gradients, not a region where "Love and Fear both hold." Distinguish it from Submission's gain-dominance modes: those are imbalances; the trapped form is mutual lock. Its remedy is structural — the corrective/transmissive work of Reconciliation — not more or less of either primitive.

    Related

    Submission = Love ⊕ Fear — the Force whose canonical failure this is. Reconciliation = Apology ⊕ Gratitude — the work whose absence produces the trap. Opposed-Gradient Contention — the coupling-mode whose lock-up this is. The Sentimental Form — Reconciliation's parallel canonical failure. Love = (toward, relational gap) — "leaving destroys relation" — the toward-gradient that locks. Fear = (away, boundary violation) — "leaving destroys safety" — the away-gradient that locks.

    Source

    Part III of the treatise ("Submission = Love ⊕ Fear, by opposed-gradient contention"). Provenance: canonical for the named pathology. The treatise also notes the seed independently flags "fear-governed operation" as Fear-gain-dominance under another name (seed corroboration of the gain-dominance mode).
