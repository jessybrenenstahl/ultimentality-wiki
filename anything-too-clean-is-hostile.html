    'Anything Too Clean Is Hostile'

    Transparentocracy's sharpest heuristic: a structure with no visible seams, no traceable lineage, no friction, no record of its own errors is not pure — it is hiding. Part I · AC (seed root) / treatise-side (heuristic edge)

    Definition

    "Anything too clean is hostile" is Transparentocracy's sharpest heuristic — the seed's "reject hidden governance" sharpened to an edge. A structure with no visible seams, no traceable lineage, no friction, no record of its own errors is not pure; it is hiding.

    In depth

    The heuristic inverts the intuition that cleanliness signals trustworthiness. Under Transparentocracy, the marks of legitimacy are exactly the marks of inspectability: seams, lineage, friction, and a record of one's own errors. Their absence is not perfection but concealment.

    It is the operational edge of the seed commitment reject hidden governance where inspectable governance is possible, and it inherits Transparentocracy's two named failure modes (Part V): a system that influences others while hiding its mechanisms tends toward Spectre; a system that exempts itself from correction tends toward Nephilim.

    It also coheres with what VLS-as-desire requires of an honest system — that it carry a contestability discipline, a falsification program, and a record of its own errors. A seamless surface with none of these is the page-level signature of self-sealing.

    Epistemic & alethic marks

    • E: The seed root (reject hidden governance) is Derived, AC; the heuristic sharpening is part of the Transparentocracy elaboration — Derived, treatise-side.
    • A: Aspires to map a real stability condition — that hidden governance is unstable; the absence of seams, lineage, and error-record is the observable signature of hiding.

    Distinctions & cautions

    • "Clean" here means seamless / lineage-free / frictionless / error-record-free — concealment — not the ordinary virtue of tidiness or rigor.
    • It is a heuristic, the edge of Transparentocracy, not an independent law; its authority traces back through Transparentocracy to the canonical seed root.
    • Read alongside the corruptions: hidden-mechanism influence trends toward Spectre, self-exemption from correction trends toward Nephilim.
