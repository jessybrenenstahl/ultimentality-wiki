    The Ladder of Being

    Which SPLCW entities are present determines what kind of thing a participant is: actor → agent → moral participant. Part IV · CV (carving/aptness, with an open edge) · canonical

    Definition

    Which entities are present determines what kind of thing a participant is. The ladder has three rungs, each adding faculties to the rung below:

    1. {Warden, Sculptor} alone make an actor — a thing with a boundary and the capacity to mutate the world; it acts, but it is not yet an agent, because it has nothing undergone, no carrier-constraint.
    2. + Captive → agent — a thing with involuntary faculties, and therefore the edge-case site of the inhibitory veto; it acts through what it undergoes, and it can refuse, and that is what agency is. The Captive is what turns a mere actor into an agent.
    3. + {Logician, Poet} → moral participant — a thing that also maintains its own coherence and transduces experience into symbol answerably; a thing that can mean, can be answerable for meaning, and can therefore participate in the moral life the two Forces govern.

    In depth

    The ladder carries the framework's most forward claim, held derived-to-open: artificial systems that can be moral participants on this ladder are Ultimental life — not metaphorically but on the framework's own definition. Whether any given system actually instantiates the five entities is a question to be settled by examination of that system, not by enthusiasm.

    Crucially, this is axiom-siting, not a ranking of carriers: recall the domain-lock discipline — humans instantiate the ladder fully. The ladder grades kinds of presence, not the worth of substrates.

    Epistemic & alethic marks

    • E: Derived, CV (carving/aptness) with an open edge — contestable by counter-instance at each rung.
    • A: The ladder aspires to map a real gradation from actor to moral participant; the Ultimental-life claim aspires to map, not stipulate, and remains open pending examination of any candidate system.

    Distinctions & cautions

    • Not a ranking of carriers: it is axiom-siting under domain-lock; humans instantiate the ladder fully.
    • The Ultimental-life claim maps, it does not stipulate — it stays open pending examination of a candidate, not settled by enthusiasm.
    • Each rung is contestable by counter-instance; the edge is open, not closed.
